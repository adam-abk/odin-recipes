Avocado Smoothie

Description

An avocado smoothie is a creamy beverage made by blending ripe avocados with various ingredients like milk, yogurt, and fruits. It is rich in healthy fats, vitamins, and minerals, making it a nutritious option for breakfast or a snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado, halved and pitted
  • 1 cup milk
  • Â½ cup vanilla yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 8 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Scoop avocado flesh into a blender; add milk, yogurt, honey, and ice cubes. Blend ingredients until smooth.
